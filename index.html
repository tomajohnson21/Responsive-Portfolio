About Me

I have lived in the Chicagoland area my entire life, having been raised in Naperville and graduating high school from Neuqua Valley High. I then attended Loyola University Chicago to study Psychology and Pre-Med. This however was not the path for me. After a few years on and off with classes and working at Loyola, I ultimately decided to pursue a career in web development at Northwestern University's bootcamp.
